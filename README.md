#  Global EV Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
This project presents an advanced Power BI dashboard built using the IEA Global EV dataset. The dashboard analyzes electric vehicle trends across regions, powertrains, and time to generate actionable insights.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze global EV stock and sales growth
- Compare EV adoption across regions
- Understand powertrain distribution (BEV, PHEV, FCEV)
- Evaluate charging infrastructure and electricity demand
- Identify key trends in EV adoption over time

---

##  Key Metrics
- Total EV Stock: 4.32 Billion
- Latest Year EV Value: 2.64 Billion
- Total EV Sales: 365.49 Million
- Total Charging Points: 117.59 Million

---

##  Key Insights

### 🌎 Regional Insights
- China dominates EV adoption with ~0.97bn EV sales contribution
- Europe and USA are the next leading regions
- India shows emerging growth potential but still lower compared to global leaders

###  Category & Mode Insights
- Cars dominate EV adoption across all regions
- Vans and trucks have comparatively lower adoption rates
- Regional variation exists in vehicle category usage

###  Powertrain Insights
- BEV (Battery Electric Vehicles) dominate with ~78% share
- PHEV contributes around ~21%
- FCEV has minimal adoption (~0.48%)

###  Trend Analysis
- EV adoption has increased significantly post-2020
- Strong exponential growth in EV stock and sales
- Developed regions show faster early adoption, while developing regions are catching up

###  Infrastructure Insights
- EV charging points are growing but still lag behind EV adoption rate
- Electricity demand for EVs is increasing steadily

##  Conclusion
The analysis highlights rapid EV adoption globally, led by China and dominated by BEVs.
It also reveals a gap between EV growth and charging infrastructure expansion, indicating opportunities for future development.
Developing regions show strong growth potential in the coming years.
